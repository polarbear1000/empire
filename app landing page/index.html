OUR MISSION

To eradicate poverty within the kasarani area through giving skills and knowledge to the community at large

to see skilled and knowledgeable person coming out of the kasarani community

our vision

to empower the community to acquire , demonstrate,artiulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as lifelong learners to participate and contribute to the global world and practice core values of the school:respect ,tolerance and inclusion.

why empire ?

empire is like owning something big.we wanted the community to own the school and to be in control of it whereby the community could come and acquire skills.

through this the community itself will bringdevelopement and growth at societal level thus an empowered community is a succesfeul one.

the scope about Empire

Genesis

the empire community collage came to life in 2022 around august when Maureen and isabellah shared skills and ideas that wold help kasarani community. This was after their visit to Budalangi formal settlement and found that the community had a lot of people who were vulnerable in terms of skills and knowledge that could be attained through education and earn them a living.

The Birth of empire

november 1st , the school was born and it established its base along Redsoil road in Mwiki opening , a freshly pointed cosmotology & computer class for learners.

the cosmetology teacher being Ms Isabellah while the computer being Ms Maureen, both being trustees of the school.

location

Empire is located along Redsoil Road , Mwiki ward Kararani consituency, ruaraka sub-county in nairobi.
